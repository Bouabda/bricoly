About us

Bricoly is the leading website for connecting individuals and housing professionals. Today, we are driven by the same objective: to offer individuals and artisans the most reliable and easiest way to identify, prepare and carry out all work projects.

Why is it so great?

The professionals at Bricoly are proud of their work and their relationship with the client. To demonstrate their commitment to service and quality, all professionals who wish to join Bricoly must accept the following policy.

Treat your customers with respect in all circumstances, both on the website and in person.

Clean up behind you when the job is done. Act like a responsible professional with individuals, without physical or verbal violence. We ensure mutual respect, which is why Bricoly also reminds individuals of their responsibilities.


Respect the agreements concluded and your meetings with individuals in all circumstances

We understand that as an entrepreneur you can be busy and that it is sometimes difficult for you to honor your commitments. However, be sure to notify your clients in advance to apologize and arrange a new appointment.


Clearly indicate the total amount of the site and any additional costs, as well as the start date of the site and its duration.

Individuals seek clarity and certainty and do not appreciate the unexpected. You are the expert and the individual has confidence in the image you send back. Make sure to communicate clearly and quickly your new schedule in case of unforeseen events.


Be sure to write professional messages to show your interest.

When individuals embark on a major investment in their housing, they hope for a professional quote. Therefore, be sure to write in understandable French. Don't offer ridiculously low rates. Do not use the argument of the fees of Bricoly in relation to the connection in response to an individual. Your quotes must be clear and include VAT and the method of payment.


Provide quality work, complete the job, and fix any problems if necessary.

The most important thing is to provide high quality work. Do not accept projects that you cannot carry out. Finish your work cleanly and do not abandon the work in progress. A problem may arise and cause a complaint. It is very important to settle this correctly with the individual. Can't seem to find common ground? We are at your disposal.
